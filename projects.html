Full Stack Web Application MindJournal

MindJournal is an all-in-one journaling solution. With MindJournal, users can jot down their experiences, situations and lessons and associate them with a certain emotion that describes how they felt at that moment. Users can filter by specific emotions or search entries and better map out their journey by using their previous journals to help better their future however they please.
